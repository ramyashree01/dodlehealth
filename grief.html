Introduction The International Prolonged Grief Disorder Scale (IPGDS) is a self-report test created by the WHO ICD-11 Working Group, to diagnose Prolonged Grief Disorder. Prolonged Grief Disorder, also known as Complicated grief, was recently added to the list of disorders listed in ICD-11. It is characterized by a feeling of longing and excessive preoccupation with the deceased and has a very particular set of symptoms.
